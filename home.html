Team workflow

A shared draft queue, with clear ownership throughout.

Not a PDF archive. A working queue for reports in progress - each one owned by a specific person, passed for review or reassigned with a note. The whole team sees where every report is at any time.

👤
Per-person sections, clear at a glance
Every team member has their own section on the drafts page, split into three groups: Drafts, Sent for review, and Reviews. Anyone on the team can see where every report sits at a glance.
🔁
Request review, reassign, or cancel - with notes
Pass a draft with an optional note. It moves to the recipient's Reviews section, locked to them. They return it, pass it on, or reassign it. Only the current holder can open and edit.
📋
Full comment history on every draft
Every action - request, return, reassign - is logged with a timestamp and the note left at the time. The full history is visible to anyone on the team, on any draft.
🗑
Not storing your client data
Drafts are a temporary working space. Finished reports are downloaded as PDFs and kept wherever your team already stores files. Nothing is archived on Condit's servers.

How it works

Create a report in minutes.

Choose a template, complete the guided condition report, upload photographs, and generate a branded PDF. Share, review or approve - then re-open and update at any time.

Choose a template
Every report starts from an admin-approved template. Your team sees only the forms you've published - no blank pages, no improvisation.
Complete the guided condition report
Work through the structured form during the examination. Tick checklists, select from dropdowns, rate condition, add notes.
Upload photographs
Batch-upload photos in one action and add captions. They're embedded alongside the assessment in the final report.
Generate a branded PDF
One click generates a branded PDF with your logo, colours and photos embedded. No formatting work.
Share, review or approve
Share directly to email or WhatsApp, or send the draft to a colleague for review with a note. They return it, approve it, or pass it on.
Re-open and update at any time
Upload any Condit PDF and every field, photo, and note repopulates instantly. Correct a detail, add notes, or use it as the basis for the next report on the same work.
