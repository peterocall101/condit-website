Cookie Policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026

This policy explains the cookies and similar technologies (such as browser local storage) used by condit.co.uk and the Condit application at condit.app.

In short: Condit uses only strictly necessary cookies and local storage that are essential to sign you in and run the app. We do not use advertising or analytics tracking cookies. Because of this, under the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) we are not required to show a consent banner. This will change if we add analytics or other non-essential cookies, and we will update this policy and seek consent if so.

Third-party content

We load fonts from Google Fonts and software libraries from jsDelivr. These do not set cookies on your device, but the providers receive your IP address in order to deliver the content. See our Privacy Policy for more on third parties.

Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies and clear local storage through your browser settings. Because the items above are essential, blocking them will prevent you from signing in to or using Condit.

Changes

We will update this policy if our use of cookies changes. Please check back from time to time.
